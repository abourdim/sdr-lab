📟 micro:bit Radio Guide

micro:bit has a built-in 2.4 GHz radio — perfect for kids' first radio experience!

What You Need

  • Two micro:bit boards (for sending and receiving)
  • USB cables for programming
  • Computer with web browser

Key Concepts

  • Radio Groups (0-255): Like TV channels — both devices must be on the same group
  • Send/Receive: One micro:bit sends, the other receives
  • RSSI: Signal strength — move closer or further to see it change
  • Range: About 10-20 meters indoors

Programming

Use MakeCode (blocks) or MicroPython to program your micro:bit radio.

⚠️ Important: micro:bit radio groups are NOT encrypted! Anyone nearby with a micro:bit on the same group can read your messages. This is a great lesson in why encryption matters.
