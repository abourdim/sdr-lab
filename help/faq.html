📋 Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is SDR Kids Lab?

SDR Kids Lab is a free, educational web app that teaches kids and beginners about Software Defined Radio (SDR), radio waves, and wireless communication through fun interactive lessons and simulated labs.

Do I need any hardware?

No! The app works entirely in simulation mode. However, for hands-on experience, we support micro:bit, ESP32+LoRa, RTL-SDR, and HackRF One.

Is this app free?

Yes, SDR Kids Lab is completely free and open source for educational use.

Does this app collect my data?

No. SDR Kids Lab is privacy-first and local-only. All your progress and settings are stored on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

Learning

What are the three levels?

🟢 Newb: Simple explanations with fun analogies — perfect for beginners.
🟡 Explorer: Technical vocabulary and deeper understanding.
🔴 Developer: Advanced architecture, formulas, and best practices.

What age is this for?

The Newb level is designed for kids aged 8+. Explorer level suits ages 12+. Developer level is for advanced students, teachers, and adults.

Hardware

Why is RTL-SDR the kids' primary tool?

RTL-SDR can only receive — it cannot transmit. This makes it completely safe for kids. There's zero risk of illegal transmission.

Why is HackRF One locked?

HackRF One can transmit, which requires legal knowledge and responsibility. It's available at Developer level and for teachers only.

Technical

Does this work offline?

Full offline support is planned for v1.0.0. Currently, the app works best with an internet connection for loading fonts.

Which browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari (latest versions). Tablet browsers are also supported.
