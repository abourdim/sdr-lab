📖 How To Use SDR Kids Lab

Getting Started

Step 1: Open index.html in your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari).
Step 2: Wait for the splash screen to finish loading.
Step 3: Click "🚀 Start Adventure" to begin with Module 0, or click any module card.

Choosing Your Level

Step 4: Open the sidebar (☰ menu) and select your level: 🟢 Newb, 🟡 Explorer, or 🔴 Developer.
Step 5: You can change your level anytime — content adapts instantly!

Navigating Modules

Step 6: Use the sidebar to jump to any module, or use the Previous/Next buttons at the bottom of each module.
Step 7: Follow the breadcrumb trail at the top to see where you are.

Settings

Step 8: Click the ⚙️ gear icon to access Settings.
Step 9: Switch between 🌌 Space Dark and ☁️ Sky Light themes.
Step 10: Adjust font size, enable accessibility features, or choose your role (Student/Teacher/Parent).

Saving Progress

Step 11: Your progress is saved automatically in your browser (local storage).
Step 12: Use Export/Import in Settings to backup or transfer your progress.

Need Help?

Step 13: Click the ❓ Help button anytime to access FAQ, Glossary, and Guides.
