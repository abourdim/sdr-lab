🔒 Privacy Policy

SDR Kids Lab is 100% local and private. No data is ever sent to any server.

What We Store

  • Your settings (theme, level, accessibility preferences)
  • Your learning progress (which modules you've completed)
  • Your achievements and badges
  • Your bookmarks

Where It's Stored

Everything is stored in your browser's IndexedDB — on your device only.

What We Don't Do

  • ❌ No data sent to servers
  • ❌ No analytics or tracking
  • ❌ No cookies (except browser storage)
  • ❌ No third-party services
  • ❌ No advertising
  • ❌ No personal information collected

Your Control

You can export, import, or delete all your data anytime from Settings.
