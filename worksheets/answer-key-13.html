Module 13: Security & Ethics — Answer Key

Q1: 1) Listen, don't transmit without permission 2) Keep private things private 3) Never jam or interfere 4) Know your country's rules 5) Be a good community member
Q2: Yes — receiving radio signals is generally legal in most countries (with some exceptions for encrypted comms)
Q3: Responsible disclosure means privately notifying vendors about vulnerabilities and giving them time (typically 90 days) to fix before public disclosure
Q4: Privacy and not spying on others (Quran 49:12: 'do not spy on one another')
