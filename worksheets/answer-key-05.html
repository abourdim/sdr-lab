Module 5: Antenna Lab — Answer Key

Q1: a) Dipole b) Yagi c) Monopole (also: patch, loop, parabolic, helical, etc.)
Q2: L = c / (2 × f) or L = 150/f(MHz) for half-wave dipole in meters
Q3: SWR (Standing Wave Ratio) measures how well the antenna impedance matches the feedline
Q4: dBi (decibels relative to an isotropic radiator) or dBd (relative to a dipole)
Q5: DRAW — should show two equal-length rods with feedpoint in center, feedline attached
